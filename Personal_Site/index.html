Welcome to Frem's The World The Enemies

The Premise

After 3 months in Frem's, you're finally starting to understand your new life as a scraper. Your work for the local tribes helping find items and hunting. Something is changing though, new creatures are appearing throughout the mall. If you don't find a way out soon, it will soon be too much.

The mall

The mall has no exit. The locals have tried everything they can to escape. But the walls are nothing if not indestructible. The mall, however, is stocked with anything you could ever want. "Employees" periodically restock the parts of the mall, so you'll never run out of goods.

The "employees"

The employees are the biggest threat of Frem's. No-one knows where they come from, but they never seem to end. In fact, there are more of them than ever before. To you and as well as any other tribes, they are extremely hostile. It's up to you to work around them. The customer isn't always right here.
