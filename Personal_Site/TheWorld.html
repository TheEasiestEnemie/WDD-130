The World

Layout

The mall is full of shops containing all the things you need from body armor to apples. There are several of each type of store throughout the mall, some bigger than others. There are several floors within Frem's, of which travel can be made easier through the use of hooks and ropes.

Employees in Their Place

How good the goods are is equal to what's guarding it; from simple cashiers to monstrous security. Each employee patrols it's respective area, it is just doing its job after all.

Tribal Towns

You're not the only one stuck in the giant mall, dozens of others have fallen prey to Frem's, some coming long before your time. Not all of them see eye to eye, and as such have waged war on eachother as well as the employees. You'll have to play the ambassador for different factions or work alone; your choice.
