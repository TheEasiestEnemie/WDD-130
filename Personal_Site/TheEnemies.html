Welcome to Frem's The World The Enemies

The Cashier

The first line of Employees. There are always at least half a dozen in each store. They walk their store and man the registers.

The Restocker

The restocker stocks the shelves of each store. They get their goods from the warehouse, and can be seen traveling through the halls and in the stores. Sometimes if they are carrying something valuable, they will be escorted by Security.

Security

Hulking, violent, and fast, these behemoths guard the high profile restockers and patrol the higher end storefronts like the gun and jewelry store. They are very hard to kill.

The Manager

The manager watches off the Office and occasionally goes out to find the player by the use of the security cameras. They will make their presence known, and head to the players last known location. The other employees, even Security, hide from the Manager.

Warehouse Man

He is the sole worker of the warehouse. There is a reason he is the only one. He is the equipment, Security, and Manager combined. Not even the Manager dares step into the warehouse.

The Mechanic

The Mechanic fixes broken security cameras throughout the store. They also set up traps to catch/kill the player.
